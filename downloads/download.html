Software Packages

The openGauss community releases are classified into two types: LTS and preview

· Preview release: a version for beta testing, which is released every year and supported for six months.

· LTS release: a major version, which is released every year and supported for three years.

The planned and developed features in RCx will be incorporated into the corresponding LTS version.
